Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy treatments tailored to your needs

Spine Rehabilitation

Specialized treatment for spinal conditions including back pain, neck pain, herniated discs, and post-surgical spine recovery. We use advanced techniques to restore mobility and reduce pain.

  • Spinal decompression therapy
  • Postural correction
  • Core strengthening
  • Manual therapy

Sports Therapy

Performance enhancement and injury recovery for athletes of all levels. From acute injury treatment to preventive conditioning, we help you get back in the game.

  • Sports injury rehabilitation
  • Performance training
  • Stretching programs
  • Return-to-sport protocols

Neuro Rehabilitation

Specialized care for neurological conditions affecting movement and function. Our team provides comprehensive rehabilitation for stroke, Parkinson's, MS, and other neurological disorders.

  • Balance training
  • Gait re-education
  • Motor function recovery
  • Coordination exercises

Nutrition Consultations

Personalized nutritional guidance to support your rehabilitation journey and overall wellness. Our expert dietician provides comprehensive meal planning and dietary counseling tailored to your specific needs.

  • Personalized diet plans
  • Weight management
  • Sports nutrition
  • Meal planning for recovery
